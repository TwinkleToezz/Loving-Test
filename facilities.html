Our Facilities

Explore our dedicated spaces designed for your dog's happiness and well-being.

Woodland Walks

Our private woodland walks offer your dog a chance to explore natural trails in a calm, scenic environment away from the city noise.

Indoor Play Barn

Rain or shine, our indoor play barn provides a massive, safe space for social activities and exercise in a fully climate-controlled setting.

Private Grass Areas

For dogs who prefer their own space, our private grass areas offer a secure spot for quiet relaxation and one-on-one play.

Pet Watch

Our Pet Watch hub is the heart of our safety operations, where trained staff monitor all areas of the hotel 24/7 to ensure every guest is safe and comfortable.
